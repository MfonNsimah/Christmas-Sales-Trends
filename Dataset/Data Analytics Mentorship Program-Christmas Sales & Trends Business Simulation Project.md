# Data Analytics Mentorship Program: 
## Christmas Sales & Trends Business Simulation Project

Based on the **Christmas Sales & Trends dataset**, I have designed a complete **industry-standard mentorship project framework** that simulates a real corporate analytics engagement in a retail organization.

---

# 1. Realistic Business Scenario

## Industry

FestiveMart Retail Group

**Industry:** Retail & E-Commerce Analytics

---

## Company Background

**FestiveMart Retail Group (FRG)** is a multinational retailer specializing in seasonal and holiday products sold through:

* Physical retail stores
* Online marketplace
* Mobile commerce platforms
* Corporate gifting services

The company operates across multiple geographic locations and experiences significant revenue fluctuations during seasonal campaigns, especially during the Christmas period.

## Business Problem

Following the 2023 Christmas sales campaign, management observed:

* Rising promotional expenses
* Declining customer satisfaction
* Increased product returns
* Delivery inefficiencies
* Uneven sales performance across locations
* Poor visibility into customer behavior

The executive team commissioned the Data Analytics Department to investigate the causes and provide strategic recommendations.

# 2. Business Requirements

## Business Questions

Students must answer:

### Sales

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which products underperform?
* What categories dominate holiday sales?

### Geography

* Which regions perform best?
* Which stores require intervention?

### Customers

* Who are the highest-value customers?
* Which age groups spend most?
* How does gender influence purchasing?

### Marketing

* Do promotions increase revenue?
* What promotions are ineffective?

### Operations

* Which shipping methods perform best?
* What factors drive returns?

### Experience

* What impacts customer satisfaction?
* How does delivery affect ratings?

---

### Customer KPIs

* Customer Lifetime Value

### Operational KPIs

### Marketing KPIs

* Promotion Conversion Rate
* Promotion ROI

## Business Rules

* Negative sales not allowed.
* Discount cannot exceed 50%.
* Satisfaction ranges from 1–5.
* Delivery time cannot be negative.
* Returned products generate operational costs.
* Online and offline sales analyzed separately.
